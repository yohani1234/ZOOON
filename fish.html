Fish Species

Rohu

Rohu is a large, silver-colored freshwater carp. Found in Indian rivers, it's protein-rich and a delicacy in Bengal and Bihar, often cooked in curries.

Katla

Katla, a major Indian carp, has a broad head and greyish scales. Popular in North India, it's protein-rich and low in calories, ideal for curries.

Mrigal

Mrigal is a freshwater carp with a slender body. Found in Indian rivers, it's a protein-rich aquaculture fish, popular in traditional dishes.

Pomfret

Pomfret, an Indian butterfish, has delicate white flesh. Rich in omega-3, it's a South Asian delicacy, often grilled or fried whole.

Indian Salmon

Indian Salmon, or Rawas, is an oily saltwater fish with pinkish-orange meat. Found off India's west coast, it's protein-rich and flavorful.

Hilsa

Hilsa, a Bengali delicacy, is a fatty freshwater fish with tender meat. Rich in omega-3, it's the national fish of Bangladesh.
